OpenSourceFunding

Hi folks,

We are a couple of guys who are scientifically engaged in the topic of open source. To get a better picture of the current state of play, weâ€™d really like to find out how you feel about open source related funding. The aim of the survey is to find out, whether open source developers should be involved in the commercial success of their projects or who the use of open source actually helps - the developers or the companies that make money from the use of the components? Furthermore, it is of interest, whether and if so, what effect an incentive model has on the quality and operation of the software?

As we know how precious your time is, we have kept the survey itself as compact as possible. It will be online for four weeks and the whole takes no longer than 10 minutes. The few questions are private and your answers will be evaluated anonymously. The results will of course be made available to you under opensourcefunding.github.io.

Pls take part in the survey. Every contribution is very important. It would be great if we could use the results to improve the situation for users and developers and thus secure open source projects in the long term. We personally want to thank you for every second invested in our research. You guys rock!

Cheers,

Your fellow research team
